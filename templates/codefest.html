Winterfest

About Winterfest

Winterfest is an exciting Tech ExPo style event hosted by the CS Council, aimed at providing older elementary school and middle school students with an exploratory view of STEM topics. The event features a diverse selection of booths, tables, and speakers, offering a panoramic experience of the world of technology.

Activities

Winterfest offers a range of engaging activities, including:

  • In-person booths and exhibits
  • Estimathons and other interactive challenges
  • Guest speakers from various STEM fields

Join us to experience the wonders of science and technology!
